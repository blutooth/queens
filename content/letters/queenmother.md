With warm regard and in the spirit of African unity, I am delighted to extend to you this personal invitation to attend the **African Global Queens Summit**, to be convened in the United Kingdom, with official arrival on **14 August 2026** and departure on **31 August 2026**. This landmark gathering brings together African Queens, traditional leaders, cultural custodians, and friends of Africa from across the continent and the Diaspora.

## A Celebration of African Leadership & Heritage

For generations, African royal women have served as the custodians of land, the moral anchors of their communities, and the guardians of cultural continuity. The African Global Queens Summit is convened to honour that legacy — a dignified, intentional assembly that celebrates our shared heritage and advances Africa's cultural, social, and moral presence within the global order.

## Strategic Purpose of the Summit

- Establish a permanent global platform for African Queens and cultural leaders.
- Advance unified positions on heritage, restitution, and reparative justice.
- Strengthen partnerships between Africa, the United Kingdom, Europe, and the Diaspora.
- Promote education, healthcare, women's empowerment, and sustainable development.
- Preserve royal heritage while cultivating the next generation of African leadership.

## Duration, Accommodation & Logistics
