With the highest esteem and in accordance with established international protocol, I extend to you this formal and personal invitation to attend the **African Queens and Women Cultural Leaders Summit**, to be convened in the United Kingdom from **14 to 31 August 2026** — a pioneering Pan-African initiative conceived to unite the traditional leadership of Africa and the global African diaspora.

## A Gathering of Honour

Your Excellency's statesmanship and leadership have transcended the shores of your nation. Through your advocacy for justice, equitable development and the dignity of people of African descent, you have become an inspiring voice of the Global South and a champion of the enduring bonds between Africa and the wider world. It is precisely because of this distinguished legacy that your presence at this Summit would carry such profound significance.

## A Pan-African Vision

The Summit is the first initiative of its kind, bringing together African Queens and women cultural leaders to strengthen traditional institutions, build leadership capacity and forge enduring partnerships across Africa and the diaspora. Our collective vision is to unite Africa through its traditional leadership while equipping our Queens to receive, guide and reconnect our brothers and sisters in the diaspora as they reclaim their rightful place within the African family.

## The Honour of Your Presence

Your personal attendance would lend immeasurable distinction to our deliberations. Your voice, wisdom and leadership would inspire delegates from across Africa and the diaspora and affirm our shared commitment to Pan-African collaboration. During your visit, it would be my privilege to welcome you as my guest at Hawkhill Place, my British country home, where one of the Summit events will be held — an opportunity for meaningful dialogue in an atmosphere of friendship, mutual respect and shared purpose.

There are occasions that simply deserve representation, and there are occasions that shape history through the presence of visionary leaders. It is my earnest hope that you will honour us with your own presence and join us in laying another foundation stone in the enduring relationship between Africa and the global African family.

Please accept, Your Excellency, the assurances of my highest consideration and profound respect.
