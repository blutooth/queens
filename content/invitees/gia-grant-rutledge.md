Dear Dr Grant-Rutledge,

It is with the greatest admiration and respect that I extend to you this personal invitation to attend the **African Global Queens Summit**, to be convened in the United Kingdom from **14 to 31 August 2026** — and to honour us with your presence as a distinguished **Guest Speaker**.

## A Leader of Distinction

Your remarkable career reflects a rare union of science, service, and enterprise. As a **Health Scientist with the United States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)**, with over **twenty years** of experience forging public–private partnerships across all fifty states and the U.S. territories, you have shaped programmes that improve lives at scale. A **Certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt**, you hold a Master of Public Health and are completing your Doctorate in Policy, Planning and Development, with a certificate in Social Innovation, at the University of Southern California.

## An Entrepreneur and Champion of Women

As **Founder and Chief Executive of ProEval Insights** — a Woman-Owned Small Business — and of **Songbird Farm at Chattahoochee Hills**, and as co-founder of **HelloGrants**, you have devoted your gifts to empowering women entrepreneurs, advancing sustainable and urban agriculture, and strengthening communities. Your service on the CDC's Preventing Chronic Disease editorial board, the Chattahoochee Hills Charter School Foundation, and the USDA Urban Agriculture Committee speaks to a life of purposeful, principled leadership.

## An Invitation to Speak

It would be our profound honour to welcome you to the Summit as a **Guest Speaker** — to share your wisdom on public health, women's economic empowerment, social innovation, and sustainable development with African Queens, traditional leaders, and changemakers from across the continent and the Diaspora. Your voice would inspire our delegates and enrich the very vision this Summit seeks to advance.

## Duration, Accommodation and Logistics

Invitees are respectfully requested to reserve the full period **14–31 August 2026** to ensure meaningful participation across all Summit engagements. The Secretariat will be glad to arrange accommodation in the United Kingdom based on your requirements, following your confirmation of attendance.

Dr Grant-Rutledge, your presence and your voice would lend immeasurable distinction to this historic gathering. I look forward to welcoming you personally to the United Kingdom.
