20 May 2026
Your Excellency, Ndemupelila Netumbo Nandi-Ndaitwah
Re: African Queens’ Summit  - 14th to 31st August 2026 - Oxford and London
I write to you with the utmost respect and admiration to extend a personal invitation for you to attend the forthcoming African Queens Summit in England, where we would be deeply honoured to host you as our Keynote Speaker at University of Oxford, Mathematics Institute Oxford  on either of 17th or 18th of August 2026.
Your distinguished career—marked by steadfast leadership, your enduring commitment to Namibia’s liberation legacy, and your tireless advocacy for gender equality and international diplomacy—stands as a powerful testament to principled governance. Your role in advancing women’s representation in leadership, alongside your contributions to peacebuilding and regional cooperation, continues to inspire a new generation of African women to lead with courage, intellect, and integrity.
It is precisely this remarkable blend of vision, resilience, and dedication to inclusive progress that aligns with the spirit of the African Queens Summit. The gathering seeks to celebrate and amplify the voices of women who are shaping the continent’s future, and your presence would profoundly elevate this mission.
We would be privileged for you to share your insights, experiences, and vision as our keynote speaker, inspiring delegates from across Africa and the diaspora. Your voice carries both the wisdom of history and the promise of transformation—an influence that would resonate deeply with all in attendance.
We sincerely hope you will honour us with your presence at this important convening in England.
With highest consideration and respect.
