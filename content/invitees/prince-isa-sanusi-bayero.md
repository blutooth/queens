Your Highness,

**Re: Special Royal Invitation to the African Queens Summit 2026, United Kingdom**

On behalf of the African Queens Summit Organising Committee, it is with profound honour and the deepest respect that we extend this special invitation to Your Highness to grace the **African Queens Summit 2026**, to be held from **14th to 31st August 2026** in London and Oxford, United Kingdom.

The African Queens Summit is an esteemed international gathering that celebrates visionary leadership, cultural heritage, and excellence among distinguished leaders from Africa and the global diaspora. We would be greatly honoured by the presence of a prince whose life has been devoted to the service, protocol, and cultural dignity of one of Africa's most illustrious royal houses.

## A Distinguished Royal Lineage

Your Highness is a scion of the ancient and revered Kano Emirate — son of the late **Khalifah Sir Muhammadu Sanusi I**, Emir of Kano, and grandson of **Emir Abdullahi Bayero**. Born of this distinguished lineage, you have carried the honour of the Kano royal house with grace, loyalty, and unwavering devotion across a lifetime of service.

## A Life of Service and Achievement

- A **trained pilot and aviator** of national renown, having flown at the service of **five former Nigerian Heads of State** — a rare distinction reflecting the trust placed in your skill and discretion.
- **Chief Protocol Officer and Principal Private Secretary** to the Emirs of Kano, serving with distinction over decades as the custodian of palace protocol, court tradition, and royal ceremony.
- A respected **keeper of Kano's cultural heritage** and court customs, widely regarded as a living repository of the Emirate's history and etiquette.
- A **polyglot** — conversant in English, French, Arabic, and Hausa — and a participant in the **Doha Conference on Inter-Cultural and Civilisation Dialogue**, reflecting a lifelong commitment to bridging cultures.

## An Honoured Invitation

It would be our privilege to welcome Your Highness as a **distinguished Royal Guest**, whose experience in diplomacy, protocol, and cultural stewardship would greatly enrich the Summit and inspire delegates drawn from royalty, government, and civil society across the continent and the diaspora.

The Secretariat will be glad to arrange accommodation and protocol support in the United Kingdom, following Your Highness's confirmation of attendance.

We sincerely hope that Your Highness will honour us with your presence, and allow us the privilege of celebrating your distinguished service to the Kano royal house and to the cause of cultural diplomacy.

Please accept, Your Highness, the assurances of our highest esteem and profound respect.
