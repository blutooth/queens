Dear Mayor Brown,

It is with the greatest admiration and respect that I extend to you this personal invitation to attend the **African Global Queens Summit**, to be convened in the United Kingdom from **14 to 31 August 2026** — and to honour us with your presence as a distinguished **Guest Speaker**.

## A Historic Leader

At just thirty-one years of age, you made history as the **youngest Mayor in the history of Compton, California**, serving from 2013 to 2021. Guided by your visionary "Vision for Compton," you helped transform a city long defined by adversity into a model of renewal — a testament to courageous, community-rooted leadership.

## A Record of Transformation

Under your leadership, Compton attracted **over three billion dollars in new investment** and **halved unemployment** through sustainable, community-benefiting job growth. Through a holistic gang-intervention strategy — achieved without diverting funds to policing — the city recorded a **64% reduction in homicides**, and you personally mediated peace between rival organisations, later securing federal support through the United States Department of Justice. You founded **Compton Empowered** to turn former gang members into agents of peace, and established **one of the nation's largest Guaranteed Basic Income pilots** — pioneering ideas the world is only now beginning to embrace.

## An Invitation to Speak

It would be our profound honour to welcome you to the Summit as a **Guest Speaker** — to share your wisdom on urban renewal, economic empowerment, public safety, and transformational leadership with African Queens, traditional leaders, and changemakers from across the continent and the Diaspora. Your voice would inspire our delegates and affirm the very vision this Summit seeks to advance.

## Duration, Accommodation and Logistics

Invitees are respectfully requested to reserve the full period **14–31 August 2026** to ensure meaningful participation across all Summit engagements. The Secretariat will be glad to arrange accommodation in the United Kingdom based on your requirements, following your confirmation of attendance.

Mayor Brown, your presence and your voice would lend immeasurable distinction to this historic gathering. I look forward to welcoming you personally to the United Kingdom.
