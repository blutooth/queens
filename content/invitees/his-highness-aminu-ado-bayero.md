Your Majesty,

**Re: Special Royal Invitation to the African Queens Summit 2026, United Kingdom**

On behalf of the African Queens Summit Organising Committee, it is with profound honour, the deepest respect, and immense admiration that we extend this special invitation to Your Majesty to grace the **African Queens Summit 2026**, to be held from **14th to 31st August 2026** in London and Oxford, United Kingdom.

The African Queens Summit is an esteemed international gathering that celebrates visionary leadership, cultural heritage, social transformation, and excellence among distinguished leaders from Africa and the global diaspora. The Summit seeks to foster meaningful dialogue, strengthen international partnerships, and inspire future generations through the wisdom and accomplishments of exceptional individuals whose lives exemplify service, dignity, and impact.

## A Revered Traditional Ruler

Your Majesty stands as one of the most revered traditional rulers on the African continent. Your remarkable leadership as the **Emir of Kano** has continued the illustrious legacy of the Kano Emirate — one of Africa's oldest and most influential traditional institutions. Through your unwavering commitment to peace, unity, education, cultural preservation, youth development, and humanitarian advancement, Your Majesty has become a symbol of stability, wisdom, and national cohesion.

The African Queens Summit recognises with admiration your tireless efforts in promoting peaceful coexistence among diverse communities, strengthening traditional governance structures, advocating educational advancement, preserving the rich cultural heritage of the Kano Emirate, and serving as a bridge between tradition and modern development. As the son of the late and highly respected Emir, His Royal Highness Alhaji Ado Bayero, Your Majesty has carried forward a distinguished legacy of service while establishing your own remarkable record of leadership, diplomacy, compassion, and dedication to the welfare of your people.

## A Distinguished Honour

In recognition of these exceptional achievements and your enduring commitment to the advancement of society, we are deeply honoured to confer upon Your Majesty the:

**African Queens Summit Grand Royal Leadership and Cultural Legacy Award 2026**

This distinguished honour is bestowed upon eminent leaders whose influence transcends borders and whose lives embody excellence, integrity, cultural stewardship, and transformative leadership. We would be privileged to welcome Your Majesty as our **Royal Guest of Honour**, where your presence would greatly enrich the Summit and inspire delegates from around the world.

## The Summit Programme Will Include

- Global Leadership and Governance Forums
- Royal and Traditional Leadership Dialogues
- Education and Youth Empowerment Sessions
- Cultural Heritage and Diplomacy Roundtables
- Community Development and Humanitarian Impact Discussions
- International Partnership and Investment Engagements
- Awards and Recognition Gala Ceremony
- High-Level Networking and Cultural Exchange Events

We sincerely hope that Your Majesty will honour us by accepting this invitation and allowing us the privilege of celebrating your extraordinary contributions to humanity, leadership, and the enduring legacy of African traditional institutions.

Please accept, Your Majesty, the assurances of our highest esteem, profound respect, and distinguished consideration.
